SEPTEMBER 15, 1993 | FEDERAL TECHNOLOGY DIGEST

ADDITIONAL GUIDANCE RELEASED FOR OMRA SYSTEM ALIGNMENT

By Technology Policy Team

Recent guidance materials issued to regional and municipal administrators outline updates on OMRA integration timelines.

The documents emphasize uniform standards, performance benchmarks, and expected communication practices across agencies.

“Shared functionality is key to sustained alignment,” a spokesperson said.

The transition continues across most coverage areas.
